Pro Evolution Soccer 2014 (PSP)

Get ready to immerse yourself in the world of football like never before with KONAMI's "Pro Evolution Soccer 2014" (PES 2014). This game introduces a revolutionary experience, powered by the stunning FOX Engine, which is seamlessly combined with a groundbreaking soccer system that places the ball at the core of the action, creating an extraordinary gaming experience.





The Power of the FOX Engine

PES 2014 is a game-changer in the world of football video games, and it all starts with the FOX Engine. This powerful engine is responsible for the game's breathtaking visuals and realistic gameplay. It's the driving force behind the game's cutting-edge graphics and physics, setting a new standard for football gaming.

The FOX Engine brings players closer to the action than ever before, making every match feel like a live broadcast. It's not just a game; it's an immersive football experience. The level of detail in player models and stadiums is nothing short of astounding, and it elevates the realism of the game to unprecedented heights.

Putting the Ball at the Heart of the Action

PES 2014 is not just about playing football; it's about feeling the game at its core. The revolutionary soccer system introduced in this edition puts the ball in the spotlight.

The ball's physics and interactions are meticulously simulated to provide players with an authentic football experience. The way the ball moves, bounces, and reacts to player actions is remarkably lifelike, making every pass and shot a unique and exciting moment.

For the first time in the series, "Pro Evolution Soccer" places a strong emphasis on the importance of ball control and decision-making. This change ensures that the outcome of each match is determined by the player's skill and strategy, creating a more dynamic and engaging experience for football enthusiasts.

An Overhaul in Every Aspect

KONAMI has left no stone unturned in redefining the PES series with PES 2014. Every element of the game has been rebuilt from the ground up. The new physics systems ensure that player movements and interactions with the ball are incredibly realistic, enhancing the gameplay in ways that were previously unimaginable.

Custom team and player AI are introduced to make the in-game actions of footballers smarter and more intuitive. This not only adds depth to the gameplay but also creates a more immersive experience, making it feel as if you're controlling real football teams on the pitch.






Moreover, the game has taken a huge leap forward in animations and atmosphere. The attention to detail in player animations, such as dribbling, shooting, and celebrations, contributes to the overall authenticity of the game. The atmosphere in stadiums is electric, thanks to enhanced crowd animations and chants, which add to the excitement of every match.

A New Era in Football Gaming

"Pro Evolution Soccer 2014" is a testament to the commitment of KONAMI to deliver a truly groundbreaking football gaming experience. With the FOX Engine, the game's focus on the ball, and the comprehensive overhaul of all gameplay elements, PES 2014 ushers in a new era in the world of football video games.
