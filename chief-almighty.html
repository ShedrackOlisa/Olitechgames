Chief Almighty

Prepare to embark on a thrilling journey to an ancient world filled with ferocious beasts, unyielding fire, and enduring stone. In the epic war for survival, will you rise to become the Chief Almighty?



The stage is set in the Jurassic era, a time when colossal dinosaurs roamed the earth, and your tribe's survival depends on your leadership. Dive into a world where alliances with rival Chiefs are the key to prosperity, where nurturing and feeding dinosaurs are your way of life.

Build an unparalleled dinosaur army and establish your dominance. Hunt legendary creatures, prove your might, and face the nerve-wracking test of survival. Are you ready for this prehistoric challenge?

An Epic Strategy Mobile Game

Chief Almighty is a mobile game that takes you on an enthralling journey back to the Stone Ages. You'll explore vast continents teeming with players from around the world, and the epic adventure begins with the hunt for archaic beasts, leading to a savage and primeval frenzy of hunting. Harvest resources, build strategic alliances with Chiefs, and fortify your tribe to ensure your survival.

Alliances and Battles

Alliances, raids, territorial expansion, and occupancy are the building blocks of power in this world. Augment your influence with mighty allies, prepared to face the most worthy of Chiefs in head-to-head battles. The question remains: will diplomacy or domination prevail? Trust your primal instincts, for only the courageous and ingenious will rule this continent.

Legendary Features

Real-Time Strategic Rallies
Mobilize your clan comrades, marshal primordial behemoths, awaken your leadership skills, and lead warriors and ally Chiefs to claim the title of the Almighty Chief. In this world, real-time strategy is the key to triumph.

Extraordinary Graphics
Chief Almighty's HD Unity3D Game Engine presents lifelike details in a wide-angle panoramic map. Every nuance of this vibrant world is fully realized, immersing you in its beauty and danger.

Global Server
In this global server, players from diverse cultures compete fiercely for revered relics, all in pursuit of the exalted Arch-Chief's honor. The world of Chief Almighty is a tapestry of mystic ancient relics and treasures of immeasurable wealth. Join a clan and engage in primal, compelling battles, no matter your origins.

Clash of Warriors and Dinosaurs
Command formidable warriors and Jurassic dinosaurs. Barbarians bring glory to your clan, with unyielding courage that even formidable Behemoths fear. Javelineers strike fear into enemy ranks with extraordinary precision and agility.

War Riders, mounted on swift dinosaurs, descend like a storm, wreaking chaos and destruction. Behemoths, born of a time long past, serve as a formidable army under your command, including agile wild boars, enormous mammoths, triceratops, and a host of other prehistoric beasts.

Explore More Dino Features
In Chief Almighty, you'll discover a wealth of functions: dine your dinosaurs, boost their well-being, train them, and establish your own dinosaur army. Become a Dino savant and gain ultimate dominance in the mesmerizing Jurassic world.
