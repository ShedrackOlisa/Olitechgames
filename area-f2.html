Area F2

The first Close-Quarters Battle (CQB) shooting game on mobile! Global Launch Event: ALL AGENTS FREE for 7 days (Until May 31, 2020)

In Area F2, players engage in Close-Quarters Battle (CQB) combat, immersing themselves in ultra-realistic environments as either attackers or defenders.

Choose from a wide selection of elite agents, each with their unique special ability. Every wall and roof in the game is destructible or can be reinforced, adding a layer of challenge as strategies need to adapt to the dynamic environment.


Features

  1. 20+ Agents with Exclusive Gadgets: Select from a roster of 20+ elite agents, all inspired by real-life special forces. Each agent possesses a unique gadget, allowing for a plethora of tactical combinations and interactions.

    Lay down suppressing fire with a machine gun, destroy walls using explosive crossbow bolts, or set trip mines at potential entry points.
  2. Realistic Scenes with Destructible Walls: Navigate varied and realistic environments that are fully destructible.

    Attackers can create entry points by destroying walls, while defenders can reinforce doors and windows to fortify against attacks.
  3. Deploy Drones to Gather Intelligence: Gain a tactical advantage by deploying drones to scout the area or disrupt enemy communication, aiding in target identification. Information warfare plays a crucial role in achieving victory.
  4. Multi-Dimensional Combat Approaches: Utilize the environment in a 3D battlefield—climb walls to attack from above or breach ceilings to strike from below. The game offers multi-dimensional combat strategies.
