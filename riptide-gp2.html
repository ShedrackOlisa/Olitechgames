Riptide GP2

Race across dynamic waves on rocket-powered watercraft in Riptide GP2, the sequel to the acclaimed racing game Riptide GP. Developed by Vector Unit, this high-speed water racing game takes everything to the next level, offering players an exhilarating and visually stunning experience.



Game Features:

  1. Challenge Your Friends: Compete against your friends' best times in the exciting VR Challenge mode.
  2. All New Career Mode: Engage in a variety of events, including Race, Hot Lap, Elimination, and Freestyle, to earn XP and cash for upgrading your hydro jet, unlocking new stunts, and enhancing your rider's performance.
  3. All New Watercraft: Collect 9 powerful hydro jets and customize their performance and colors to gain an edge over your competition.
  4. All New Stunt System: Unlock and master 25 outrageous stunts to wow the crowd, charge your boost, and leave your competitors in your wake.
  5. Game the Way You Want: Enjoy seamless support for multiple control configurations, including tilt, touch-screen, and gamepad play.
  6. Google Play Game Services: Earn achievements and keep your game progress synced to the cloud with your Google account.
  7. Cutting-Edge Tech: Powered by the Vector Engine 4, Riptide GP2 features stunning HD graphics and a fast-paced racing experience.

Customer Support:

If you encounter any issues while running the game, please contact support@vectorunit.com with details of your device, Android OS version, and a description of the problem. They guarantee to resolve your issue or provide a refund. However, leaving problems in a review won't help you get support.

For quick solutions to common issues, visit Vector Unit Support.
