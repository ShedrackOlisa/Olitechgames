Gangstar Vegas


Gangstar Vegas: World of Crime, you'll step into a world of intrigue and danger, battling against gangsters and the mafia to establish your dominance as the leader of Las Vegas.



Experience an era of intense exploration and chaos in the heart of Vegas:

It's an open-world extravaganza: Immerse yourself in thrilling gunfights, high-speed races, and a world of criminal activities.

Over 80 levels await: Take on more than 80 missions packed with adrenaline-pumping racing, intense shootouts, and third-person shooter (TPS) action.

Arm yourself with advanced weaponry: Gear up with an arsenal that includes sniper rifles, lasers, rockets, and much more. Gangstar Vegas is your gateway to the life of a gang leader in Las Vegas, navigating the treacherous waters of gang warfare and encounters with underworld figures in a free, open-world setting.
Vegas, the city of sin, is yours to explore. Engage in various TPS missions, confront mafia cartels, compete for ultimate supremacy, and become entwined in the world of adventure, crime clans, and rival gangs.

This is an epic adventure saga where the world of mafia and gang warfare is your stage. Stay on your toes with extra missions, seasonal updates, and limited-time events.

In the heart of this gangster world, you'll find street fights and mafia dealings aplenty, along with thrilling six-gun action missions. Enjoy fight-night boxing, street battles at any location, and a variety of city driving experiences with numerous vehicles at your disposal.

Prepare for open-world encounters that bring a diverse range of racing challenges with various vehicles, collectible weapons, and clothing.

Engage in grand theft auto escapades on the bustling streets of Las Vegas while taking on rival gangsters. Motorcycles, trucks, and boats are just a few of the options you have for fulfilling your mission objectives.


Vice runs deep through the streets and neighborhoods, with gunfire echoing around every corner. Participate in TPS missions in Las Vegas, a city brimming with gangster activity, alien invasions, tank warfare, zombie clan assaults, and conflicts with various mafia groups.

Each mission in this adventure saga offers access to a range of vehicles and weapons. Engage in gang wars armed with Molotov cocktails, grenade launchers, and a host of other weapons and vehicles to execute your plans and missions.

Gangstar Vegas beckons you into a world where you'll trade in danger, crime, and adrenaline at every turn.
